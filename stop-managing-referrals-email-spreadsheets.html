How to Stop Managing Referrals in Email and Spreadsheets

Email and spreadsheets can track work for a while. They struggle when referral volume grows and missing information starts hiding in threads.

Get a Free Practice Audit →

Problem

Your inbox is not a referral management system.

Referral emails, attachments and follow-ups often start in the inbox. Then a spreadsheet becomes the tracker. Soon the team has two places to check, no shared status view and no reliable way to see what is overdue.

Emails hide work

Important referral details get buried in threads, forwarded messages and attachments.

Spreadsheets need constant care

They show information only after someone remembers to update them.

Status gets unclear

New, pending, in progress and complete referrals can blur together.

Better Path

Move to a visible referral workflow.

A practical Microsoft 365 workflow can capture referral details, save documents, update status, flag missing information and notify the right people. Read more about referral intake automation for allied health practices.
