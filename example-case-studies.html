Referral intake for a small allied health practice

Practice type: Small multidisciplinary clinic.

Problem: Referrals arrive by email with attachments and are copied into a spreadsheet.

Example Workflow

Missing information follow-up queue

Practice type: NDIS-adjacent provider.

Problem: Admin staff repeatedly chase missing phone, email and claim details.

Automation built: Missing field flag, tracker update and prepared follow-up email.

Tools used: Outlook, Lists, Power Automate and SharePoint.
