# AI Limitations Note

AI-assisted extraction can help prefill information from documents, but it may not always be complete or correct.

Extracted information should be reviewed before use.

AdminZero treats AI as an admin assistant, not a clinical or business decision-maker.

Recommended wording for client-facing material:

> AI-assisted extraction can help prepare referral information for review. It should not be relied on as the final source of truth, and it should not make clinical or business decisions on its own.
